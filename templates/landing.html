About AES-128

AES (Advanced Encryption Standard) is a symmetric block cipher used worldwide for secure data encryption.

This visualizer shows all steps of the encryption process including:

  • Initial round (AddRoundKey)
  • Main rounds (SubBytes, ShiftRows, MixColumns, AddRoundKey)
  • Final round (SubBytes, ShiftRows, AddRoundKey)
